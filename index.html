What Awaits

Forge Your
Pirate Legend

A fully 3D pirate experience built entirely in the browser with Three.js — no installation, no downloads. Just open and sail.

🏴‍☠️
Naval Broadside Combat

Fire synchronized 10-cannon broadsides from both sides. Watch iron shot arc through the air and crash into enemy hulls with explosive flash and flying splinters.

⚔️
Boarding & Melee

Close the gap, grapple alongside an enemy vessel, and unleash your crew in brutal deck-to-deck melee combat. Win the brawl, seize the ship, claim the loot.

🗺️
Open Caribbean World

12 unique islands — two full pirate towns with forts and taverns, volcanic peaks, tropical atolls, and rocky reefs — all charted on a live minimap.

⛴
5 Distinct Ship Classes

From the nimble Red Raptor Sloop to the fearsome Leviathan Galleon with 18 guns per side. Each vessel has unique stats, visuals, and a fearsome identity.

👥
Crew Levelling System

Your crew earns XP through hits, kills and boarding victories — levelling up to improve reload speed, accuracy, hull strength and combat instinct.

💰
Loot & Plunder

Sink enemy ships and loot spills across the waves. Collect gold, rum, powder, and rare items with a full rarity system tracked in your inventory.

🌊
Gerstner Wave Ocean

Six-frequency Gerstner wave simulation with real horizontal displacement, whitecap foam at crests, and a deep colour gradient from navy to turquoise shallows.

🤖
AI Enemy Fleet

Five enemy pirates patrol the seas. They detect you, give chase, align broadsides, fire volleys, retreat when wounded, and respawn to hunt again.

🏰
Pirate Town Islands

Tortuga and Port Royale hand-built with stone forts, timber taverns, working docks, market stalls, shanty houses, lighthouses, and glowing lanterns at dusk.

The Legend

A Golden Age
of Piracy

In the uncharted waters between Tortuga and Port Royale — where merchant galleons grow fat on New World gold and the Royal Navy dares not venture — this is where your story begins. Sail out. Find your prey. Leave nothing but wake.

Corsair's Wake is a fully 3D pirate naval combat game built from scratch using Three.js and pure JavaScript. Every ship, cannon, wave, and island has been hand-crafted — from the irregular coastlines of Tortuga to the Gerstner wave physics of the open ocean.

Three.js r128  ·  Vanilla JavaScript  ·  No frameworks  ·  No installation  ·  Any modern browser
